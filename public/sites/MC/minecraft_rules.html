Server Rules
Here are the rules for our Minecraft server:
  • Rule 1: Respect all players. Treat others with kindness and courtesy. No harassment or offensive behavior is allowed.
  • Rule 2: No cheating or hacking. Play fair and follow the server's rules regarding mods and hacks.
  • Rule 3: No griefing. Do not destroy or modify other players' builds without their permission.
  • Rule 4: No spamming. Keep the chat clean and enjoyable for everyone.
  • Rule 5: No PvP without consent. Do not engage in Player vs. Player combat without all parties' agreement.
